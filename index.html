Structure Suggestions

Search for complaint documents using a case number. **Once the case number is found, we could display a link to related documents for examiners and others with access to the site to view case specific documents that have been uploaded, but not to edit or submit.

Search for general laboratory facility information such as location, address, phone number, and director. **This could be useful in verifying labs that are the subjects of complaints.

Periodic reports of laboratory inspection status and results. **This would be linked to a report in the database that will provide statistics on completed and current investigations (say, run every month to show information about the previous 3 months). Report will not have identifiable information, but an easily accessible report will help to illustrate and share our progress.
