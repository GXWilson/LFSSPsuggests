View Case Docs

This button/link would be used to perform a search for complaint documents via a case number. Once the case number is found, we could display a link to related documents for examiners and others with access to the site to view case specific documents that have been uploaded, but not to edit or submit.

Facilities Information

I think we could develop a tool that can search for general information regarding specific facilities, such as location, address, phone number, and director. This could be useful in verifying labs that are the subjects of complaints

Recent Lab Reports

This section would contain periodic reports for laboratory status and recent, say, quarterly inspection results broken down by region.
